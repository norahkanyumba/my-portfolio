CAPE MACLEAR

Best place for getting local experiences

At the tip of Nankumba Peninsula on the southern ends of Lake Malawi, Cape maclear is both a working fishing village and a popular tourist stop.
Known locally as Chembe, this small enclave hammed in by forested mountains, has beachfront accomodation set within the community, and the proxmity
of guest chalets to home create a natural setting for travelers to interact
with residence.
